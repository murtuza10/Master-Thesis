Title: 
A small set of nuclear markers for reliable differentiation of the two closely related oak species Quercus robur and Q. petraea: PRJNA914538

Abstract:
A pool of total DNA from six individuals of Quercus petraea has been sequenced using Illumina MiSeq with 2x300 bp paired-end reads with a haploid nuclear genome coverage of 29X (GATC Biotech AG, Konstanz, Germany).
